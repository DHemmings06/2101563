WEBSITE SPECIFICATIONS

Website Theme:

  • Floral Images and Water Colour Images were used throughout the website. This was done to establish the theme of feminity and fragility.
  • Multiple Pastel Colours were also used to provide a youthful, creative, artistic feel to the website.
  • Use of forms and images to make the website user friendly and appealing to the eye
  • Pastel colours were specifically chosen to be used in this website as they are muted and add a flare of sophistication. They are also easy on the eyes and are visually appealing for the floral theme.

Target Audience:

  • This website is targeted to women as seen in the use of floral imagery and pastel colours. Both of which add a delicate, feminine look to the website, that would visually attract women.

Website Features for Intended Use:

  • Favicon Symbols for each webpage
  • Use of Images on Home Page, Personality Page and as background images
  • Use of Iframes for Personality, Storyboard and Personal Development Webpages
  • Use of Internal Links for the Navigation Bar on all webpages; Use of external links on the Home Page and Professional Webpage
